Submission Guidelines

About News Not Bombs

News Not Bombs is edited and produced by members of Canton Food Not Bombs. Canton FNB is a Pan-Left group that believes food is a right, not a privilege. We are a horizontally operated working-class collective that believes: direct action gets the goods, solidarity over charity, we keep us safe, and a meal tastes best when shared with your community.

Members and non-members are encouraged to submit relevant and creative work to the News Not Bombs quarterly zine. Credit will always be given to the author/creator by the name given.

Submission Guidelines

Acceptable Formats

Acceptable submission formats include but are not limited to: written articles, poems, comics, drawings.

Unacceptable Formats

We do not accept "AI Art" (artificial intelligence generated content), copyrighted material, or similar formats.

Unacceptable Topics

  • Calls to violence
  • Local infighting/call outs (i.e. unresolved personal conflicts within or between Leftist organizations)
  • Self promotion or promotion of other organizations (without special permission)
  • Artwork or writing deemed racist, homophobic, transphobic, xenophobic, classist, sexist, or otherwise bigoted

Print Considerations

In addition to the digital version posted on the Canton FNB website, News Not Bombs zines are printed in black and white — please keep this in mind for artwork submissions.

Keep in mind the dimensions of the zine when submitting artwork: 4.5″×7″

Written Submissions

Written submissions must be within 350–700 words.

Review Process

All submissions to News Not Bombs are either accepted or rejected by Canton Food Not Bombs' elected Committee of Propaganda; with all committee decisions subject to being modified or overturned by an official vote held by Full Membership, following inquiry and proposal.
